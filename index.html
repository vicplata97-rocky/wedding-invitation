Our Love Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favourite.

Amelia & Ethan

2019
First Meeting
It was a rainy October evening at a friend's dinner party. Ethan was the last to arrive; Amelia had saved him the last slice of pie without knowing why. They talked until midnight, long after everyone else had gone home.
2020
The First Date
A walk through the botanical gardens that turned into six hours of wandering, laughing, and forgetting they were supposed to be anywhere else. They've been inseparable ever since.
2022
Adventures Together
From the cliffs of Portugal to a tiny café in Kyoto, they built a world of shared memories — proving that the best adventures are the ones you have with the right person by your side.
2024
The Proposal
Under a canopy of stars at their favourite spot — the same botanical garden where it all began — Ethan got down on one knee. Amelia said yes before he could finish the sentence.
